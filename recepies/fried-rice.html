Fried Rice Recepie

Fried rice is the ultimate family-friendly dish that yields maximum flavor without fuss.

Loaded with tender sauteed veggies and delicious bits of scrambled egg, this top-rated recipe makes it easy to recreate a takeout favorite from home in just 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Green Peas
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Eggs
  • Leftover Rice

Steps

  1. Assemble ingredients
  2. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water
  3. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds
  4. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
